ANYWHERE WITH JESUS

1. An-y-where with Je-sus
I can safe-ly go,
An-y-where He leads me
in this world be-low ;

An-y-where with-out Him
dear-est joys would fade ;
An-y-where with Je-sus
I am not a-fraid.

2. An-y-where with Je-sus
I am not a-lone ;
Oth-er friends may fail me,
He is still my own ;

Though His hand may lead me
o-ver drear-y ways,
An-y-where with Je-sus
is a house of praise.

3. An-y-where with Je-sus
I can go to sleep,
When the gloom-y shad-ows
round a-bout me creep,

Know-ing I shall wak-en
nev-er-more to roam ;
An-y-where with Je-sus
will be home sweet home.
