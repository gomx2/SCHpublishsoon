HOW FAR FROM HOME ?

1. How far from home ?
I asked, as on
I bent my steps,
the watch-man spake ;

The long, dark night
is al-most gone,
The morn-ing soon will break.

Then weep no more,
But speed thy flight,
With Hope’s bright star
Thy guid-ing ray,

Till thou shalt reach
the realms of light,
In ev-er-last-ing day.
