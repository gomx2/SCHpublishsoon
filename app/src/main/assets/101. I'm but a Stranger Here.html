I’M BUT A STRANGER HERE

1. I’m but a stran-ger here,
Heaven is my home ;
Earth is a des-ert drear,
Heaven is my home ;

Dan-ger and sor-row stand
Round me on ev-ery hand ;
Heaven is my Fa-ther-land,
Heaven is my home.

2. What though the tem-pest rage,
Heaven is my home ;
Short is my pil-grim-age,
Heaven is my home ;

Time’s cold and win-try blast
Soon will be o-ver-past ;

3. There at my Sav-iour’s side,
Heaven is my home ;
I shall be glo-ri-fied,
Heaven is my home ;
