FATHER, WE COME TO THEE

1. Fa-ther, we come to Thee,
No oth-er help have we ;
Thou wilt our ref-uge be,
On Thee we call.

Earth is but dark and drear
With-out Thy pres-ence near ;
Be Thou our com-fort here,
Fa-ther of all.

Fa-ther, we come to Thee,
Turn not a-way ;
Help-less we come to Thee,
Hear while we pray.

2. Save from our man-y foes,
Save from our earth-ly woes ;
Be Thou our soul’s re-pose
In time of need.

Doubt-ing are we, and weak ;
To us sweet cour-age speak ;
Thy might-y arm we seek
For strength in-deed.

3. Give us Thy grace di-vine,
Seal us for-ev-er Thine ;
Our way-ward feet in-cline
From sin to flee.

Oh, guide us, we im-plore,
Till wea-ry life is o’er,
And on a bright-er shore
We dwell with Thee.
