‘TIS SO SWEET TO TRUST IN JESUS

1. ‘Tis so sweet to trust
in Je-sus,
Just to take Him at His word ;
Just to rest up-on
His prom-ise,
Just to know, “Thus saith
the Lord.”

Ref :
Je-sus, Je-sus, how I trust Him;
How I’ve proved Him
o’er and o’er !
Je-sus, Je-sus, pre-cious Je-sus !
O for grace to trust Him more !

2. O how sweet to trust
in Je-sus,
Just to trust His cleans-ing
blood ;
Just in sim-ple faith
to plunge me
‘Neath the heal-ing,
cleans-ing flood.

Of-ten when earth has
no balm for my heal-ing,
There I find com-fort,
and there I am blest.

3. Yes, ‘tis sweet to trust
in Je-sus,
Just from sin and self
to cease ;
Just from Je-sus sim-ply
tak-ing
Life, and rest, and joy,
and peace.

4. I’m so glad I learned
to trust Thee,
Pre-cious Je-sus, Sav-iour,
Friend ;
And I know that Thou art
with me,
Wilt be with me till the end.
