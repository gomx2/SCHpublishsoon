I NEED THEE, PRECIOUS JESUS

1. I need Thee, pre-cious Je-sus,
For I am ver-y poor ;
A stran-ger and a pil-grim,
I have no earth-ly store.

I need the love of Je-sus
To cheer me on my way,
To guide my doubt-ing
foot-steps,
To be my strength and stay.

2. I need the heart of Je-sus,
To feel each anx-ious care ;
To tell my ev-ery tri-al,
And all my sor-rows share.

I need the Ho-ly Spir-it
To teach me what I am,
To show me more of Je-sus,
To point me to the Lamb.

3. I need Thee, pre-cious Je-sus,
I hope to see Thee soon,
En-cir-cled with the rain-bow,
And seat-ed on Thy throne.
There, with Thy blood-
bought chil-dren
My joy shall ev-er be
To sing Thy cease-less prais-es
To gaze, my Lord, on Thee !
